MAKINABANG DENTAL CLINIC

Our dental clinic was founded in 2002 with the purpose of delivering high-quality dental care to the patients in our community. Since then, we've grown into a dental care provider known for our commitment to our patients' oral health. Our dental clinic provides a wide range of services, including oral prophylaxis (cleaning), filling, tooth extraction, dentures, orthodontic (braces) treatments and others. We intend to deliver oral healthcare that is both high-quality and cost-effective. In the years to come, we hope to continue offering excellent dental care to our patients as a trusted partner in their oral health journey.
